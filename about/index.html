About Pak Ar International

A packaging partner for businesses that need durable, presentable and customizable bags for retail, ecommerce, textile and promotional use.

Who we are

Packaging solutions built around product protection and brand presentation.

Pak Ar International works with businesses that need practical packaging with a professional finish. Our range includes poly bags, EVA frosted bags, online flyer bags, PVC bags, non-woven bags and laminated printed poly bags.

We focus on useful customization, clear communication and packaging that supports the way each product is sold, shipped or displayed.
