About Pak Ar International

Packaging solutions shaped around product needs and brand presentation.

Pak Ar International works with businesses that need reliable bags, clean finishing, practical customization and better product presentation.

Company profile

Established in 2003 with a focus on packaging quality.

The product range includes eco friendly poly bags, EVA frosted poly bags, online flyer bags, PVC bags, non woven bags and laminated printed poly bags. Each category is selected for practical commercial use across retail, apparel, courier, promotional and premium product packaging.

The goal is simple: provide packaging that looks neat, protects the product and supports brand identity through size, material, colour and printing choices.
